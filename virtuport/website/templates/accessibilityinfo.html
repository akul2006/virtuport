Accessibility Information at VirtuPort

Our Commitment

VirtuPort is dedicated to ensuring all students, faculty, staff, and visitors have equal access to digital content and facilities. Our website and campus resources comply with recognized accessibility standards to support people with disabilities.

Accessibility Features

  • Screen reader compatible web pages with descriptive alt text for images
  • Keyboard navigable website and online portals
  • Simple, consistent navigation and clear headings
  • Color contrast adhering to WCAG 2.1 AA standards
  • Captions and transcripts for video and audio materials
  • Resizable text and responsive design for various devices
  • Specialized support and services for students with disabilities

How to Request Assistance

For accessibility support, accommodations, or to report website accessibility issues, please contact our Accessibility Services team via:

  • Email: accessibility@VirtuPort.ac.in
