VirtuPort: Leading the Way in Industry-Aligned Learning and Employability

Our Mission & Vision

VirtuPort (VirtuPort) is committed to revolutionizing higher education by emphasizing skill development alongside academic excellence. Our mission is to nurture job-ready professionals and entrepreneurs equipped with real-world experience through strong industry partnerships and innovative curriculum design.

We envision VirtuPort as a beacon of quality, innovation, and inclusivity, shaping future leaders and skilled contributors to Maharashtra’s and India’s socio-economic progress.

About VirtuPort

Founded in 2022 and headquartered in Mumbai, VirtuPort is Maharashtra’s first government-established Skills University. It offers a unique blend of 60% hands-on skill training and 40% classroom instruction, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for evolving market demands.

The university boasts multiple campuses in Mumbai, Pune, Navi Mumbai, Nagpur, and Thane, offering programs across engineering, technology, business, humanities, and vocational training aligned with current and future industry requirements.

Why VirtuPort Stands Out
  • Cutting-edge curriculum co-designed with industry leaders
  • Comprehensive training in emerging domains like AI, Cybersecurity, IoT
  • Strong focus on entrepreneurship and start-up incubation
  • Modern digital infrastructure supporting experiential learning
  • Robust placement and career development ecosystem

Our Campuses

VirtuPort's campuses are strategically located to serve the state’s diverse student body:

  • Mumbai (Headquarters): Central hub with administrative and academic facilities
  • Pune: Focus on science, engineering, and research programs
  • Navi Mumbai: Business studies and digital innovation center
  • Nagpur: Skills development and vocational training
  • Thane: Emerging tech campus and community engagement
