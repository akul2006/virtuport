Admissions 2025

Your gateway to skill-based education and career readiness at MSSU

How to Apply

Follow these streamlined steps for a fast and secure online application process:

  • Create an account on MSSU's official admissions portal
  • Complete the application form with accurate personal and educational details
  • Upload necessary academic certificates and photographs
  • Pay the application fee (₹1200 for general category, ₹600 for reserved categories)
  • Submit the application and track the status via your portal dashboard

Entrance Exams Accepted

  • CUET – Undergraduate and Postgraduate Courses
  • MHT-CET – Maharashtra Common Entrance Test
  • GATE – For M.Tech and Research Programs
  • MSSU MUET – MSSU University Entrance Test
  • CAT – MBA and PGDM Programs
  • Other specified entrance exams as per program requirements

Eligibility Criteria

Program Level Eligibility Minimum Marks Required Documents
Undergraduate Programs 10+2 or equivalent with relevant stream 45% (General Category) / 40% (Reserved Categories) Academic Marksheet, ID Proof, Passport Photo
Postgraduate Programs Relevant Bachelor's Degree Minimum 50% Degree Certificate, Marksheet, ID Proof
Diploma & Certificate courses Class 10 or 12 depending on the course Varies by Course Academic Certificates & ID Proof
