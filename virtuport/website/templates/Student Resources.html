Student Resources at VirtuPort

Overview

VirtuPort provides comprehensive support to students through a variety of academic and personal resources aimed at enhancing their educational experience and career prospects.

Available Student Resources

  • Course materials, lecture notes, and recorded classes accessible online.
  • Academic calendar with key dates and deadlines.
  • Personalized academic counseling and tutoring services.
  • Career guidance, placement, and internship assistance.
  • Workshops on soft skills, communication, and interview preparation.
  • Access to digital library and research databases.
  • Student clubs, events, and cultural programs to encourage holistic growth.
  • Health and wellness programs including counseling support.
  • Facilities and support for differently-abled students.
